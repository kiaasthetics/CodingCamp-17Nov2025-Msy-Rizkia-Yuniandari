About KiiaVision

Founded in 2021, KiiaVision Indonesia began as a small and ambitious startup and has steadily evolved into one of the country’s leading technology companies. Over the years, we have expanded our expertise in software development, cloud-based solutions, and end-to-end digital transformation services, helping businesses modernize and stay competitive in a rapidly changing digital landscape.

With our main headquarters located in Jakarta and additional offices in Palembang and Yogyakarta, we are able to support clients across Indonesia. We proudly work with organizations from a wide range of industries including finance, retail, and manufacturing providing tailored technological solutions that meet their unique operational needs and long-term goals.
